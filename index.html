Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a business with no experience?

Our course is designed specifically for beginners. We guide you through idea generation, market validation, and all foundational steps so you can build your business step-by-step, even without prior experience.

How do I know if my business idea is good?

Module 2 (Idea Validation) is entirely dedicated to this. You'll learn frameworks to test your concept, talk to potential customers, and build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to gather real-world proof before spending money.

Why is market research important?

Market research helps you understand your competitors, pinpoint your ideal audience, and price your product correctly. Without it, you risk building something nobody wants or pricing it wrong.

What legal steps are involved in starting a business?

It involves choosing a structure (like an LLC or Sole Proprietorship), registering your business name, obtaining necessary licenses and permits, handling tax registration, and opening a dedicated business bank account. We break this all down in Module 6.

Do I need a website before launching?

Not always, but having an online presence builds credibility. Depending on your business type, a simple landing page or social media presence might be enough to start gathering leads before a full launch.

Can I start small and grow later?

Absolutely. In fact, we recommend it. Starting small allows you to validate your model, refine your product based on feedback, and scale only when you have proven systems in place.
